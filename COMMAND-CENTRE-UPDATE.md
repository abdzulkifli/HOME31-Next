# HOME31 V7.8.0 Command Centre Measures

The Executive Command Centre uses eight consistent headline measures:

1. Portfolio Items — count of selected-year initiative records
2. Strategic Readiness — average completeness across seven governance checks
3. Portfolio Cost Basis — total confirmed Approved Budget
4. Challenge Movement — absolute movement between Original Estimate and confirmed Post-Challenge Cost, labelled as a reduction or increase
5. Strategic Priority — records classified as strategic or corporate priority
6. At Risk — records with At Risk status or High/Extreme risk
7. Post-Challenge Cost — total confirmed Estimated Cost Post Challenge
8. Budget Coverage — percentage of records with Approved Budget confirmed

HR and ICT measures remain in Risks & Exceptions and detailed assurance views.

No database migration is required. The measures use existing initiative fields and their confirmation flags.

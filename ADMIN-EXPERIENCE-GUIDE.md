# V7.7.1 Admin Experience Design Guide

## Standard module flow

Every super-admin module follows this hierarchy:

1. Command header
2. Six to eight headline KPIs
3. Management insight or operational controls
4. Main workspace
5. Assurance and follow-up indicators

## Modules

### Executive Overview
Strategic story, portfolio health, cost, delivery, fit and decision queues.

### Enterprise Portfolio
Portfolio KPIs, selection narrative, filters, enterprise register and assurance.

### User Administration
Access KPIs, protected user creation, access governance and searchable directory.

### Risks & Exceptions
Exception KPIs, risk and concentration charts, action lanes and specialist follow-up.

### System & Future
Current foundation, design principles, roadmap, future modules and production readiness.

## Typography

- Body, data, headings and headline figures: IBM Plex Sans
- Fallbacks: Aptos, Segoe UI and Arial
- Display modes: Standard, Comfortable and Large

## Colour hierarchy

- Navy: enterprise workspace and evidence-based management
- Gold: strategic priority and key decisions
- Teal: stable, ready and integrated
- Red: critical risk and intervention
- Blue-grey: neutral data and supporting information


## V7.7.1 governance additions

- Database-enforced active-account and first-login controls
- Protected password-change and reset services
- Confirmed financial-stage handling
- Accessible initiative dialog and chart labels
- Audit logging for initiative and privileged profile changes

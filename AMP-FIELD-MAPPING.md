## Additional HOME31 fields retained

V7.2 also retains management fields not present in the Excel register:

- Executive sponsor
- Accountable owner
- Delivery lead
- HOME31 strategic pillar
- Value measure, baseline and target
- Value owner and measurement frequency
- Detailed HR, workforce and change impact
- Readiness and risk
- Evidence completeness and references
- Management review summary

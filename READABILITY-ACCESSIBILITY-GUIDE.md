# HOME31 V7.7.1 Readability & Accessibility Guide

## Default setting

The platform opens in **Comfortable** mode.

| Setting | Main body | Supporting text | Inputs | KPI values |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Standard | 15 px | 12–13 px | 15 px | 34 px |
| Comfortable | 16 px | 13–14 px | 16 px | 38 px |
| Large | 18 px | 15–16 px | 18 px | 43 px |

## Display control

The floating `Aa Display` button is available on:

- Login
- First-login password screen
- Normal-user modules
- Super-admin modules
- Initiative form

Keyboard shortcut:

`Alt + Shift + A`

## High contrast

High contrast strengthens:

- Text
- Borders
- Focus indicators
- Form controls
- Dark command-centre panels
- Chart labels

## Responsive behaviour

At smaller widths:

- Tables become labelled record cards
- Admin KPI grids collapse to one or two columns
- Initiative steps scroll horizontally
- The initiative form becomes a full-screen mobile workflow
- Display settings become a mobile-friendly panel

## Design standard

Nothing important should be displayed below 12 px in Standard mode or below
13 px in Comfortable mode.


## V7.7.1 additions

- Initiative dialog uses accessible dialog semantics.
- Focus moves into the dialog, remains inside while open and returns to the launch control.
- Escape closes the dialog.
- Icon controls and charts have accessible names.
- Personal portfolio filters have programmatic labels.
- High contrast uses dark text on light cards and white text only on dark command-centre surfaces.
